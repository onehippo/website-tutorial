Solar Centurian - Outdoor Security Light

Light up your night with a solar powered, motion detected outdoor security light!

This solar powered light is a bright (over 600 lumen) Halogen Security Light that installs easily where utility wiring is impractical or expensive. A patented passive infrared motion detector is able to detect activity up to 23m/75 feet away controls the light. When activated, the Solar Centurion throws a flood light into the target area for up to 3 minutes or as long as there is movement in the area.

It is perfect for security lighting or convenience lighting in your yard, at your garage, around your garage, or wherever security lighting is required at night!

The light is replaceable at any lighting or hardware store (Sylvania 1995/ERK miniature Halogen Lamp).
